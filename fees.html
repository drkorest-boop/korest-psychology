Rates & Coverage

Fees & Insurance

What to expect

I believe good therapy should be accessible. I accept several major insurance plans and am happy to discuss fees during our initial consultation.

If you're using insurance, I recommend calling the member services number on your card to confirm your behavioral health benefits before our first session.

BCBS Michigan Blue Care Network Out-of-Network Available

I can provide a Superbill (itemized receipt) for clients using out-of-network benefits, which you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement.
